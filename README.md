Superstore Sales Performance Analysis

 **Project Overview**
This project presents a comprehensive **Sales Performance Dashboard** built for the Superstore dataset. 
The analysis aims to help the business understand sales trends, identify top-performing products and categories, 
evaluate regional performance, and provide actionable recommendations to drive growth and profitability.

**Business Questions Answered**
- Which products generate the most revenue?
- How do sales change over time?
- Which categories and regions are most profitable?
- Where should the business focus to grow faster?

**Tools Used**
- **Power BI Desktop** (Main Dashboard Tool)
- **Power Query** (Data Cleaning & Transformation)
- **DAX** (Calculated Measures & KPIs)

**Dataset**
- **Dataset Name**: Superstore Sales Dataset
- **Source**: Kaggle
- **Key Columns**: Order Date, Category, Sub-Category, Region, Sales, Profit, Segment, Product Name

## Key Insights
- Total Sales reached **$2,297,201** with an average order value of **$458.61**.
- **Technology** category is the top performer in both Sales and Profit.
- **West region** leads in sales contribution, followed by East.
- Overall **Profit Margin** stands at **12.47%**.
- Sales show seasonal patterns with noticeable fluctuations over time.
- A small number of products (Top 10) contribute significantly to total revenue.

## Actionable Recommendations
1. **Prioritize Technology Category** – Focus inventory planning, marketing campaigns, and promotions on Technology products.
2. **Leverage West Region Success** – Study and replicate the strategies used in the West region to improve performance in Central and South regions.
3. **Improve Profitability** – Review and optimize discount strategies in low-margin sub-categories.
4. **Increase Average Order Value** – Introduce product bundling (e.g., printers + accessories) to boost revenue per order.
5. **Seasonal Planning** – Prepare stock and targeted campaigns ahead of peak sales periods.

## Dashboard Features
- **Executive KPI Cards** (Total Sales, Profit, Margin, Orders, Avg Order Value)
- **Monthly Sales Trend** analysis
- **Sales & Profit by Category**
- **Regional Performance** (Donut + Breakdown)
- **Top 10 Products** by Sales
- **Profit by Sub-Category**
- Interactive slicers (Region, Category, Segment, Order Date)
